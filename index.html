Mini Game Engine for EECS 448-007

Developed for the EECS 498 course on Game Engine Architecture at the University of Michigan, this miniature game engine utilizes C++, SDL, GLM, Lua with LuaBridge, and OpenCV. Custom features include an avatar creation tool and real-time face tracking, which enables players to integrate their own likeness into the gameplay experience seamlessly.
